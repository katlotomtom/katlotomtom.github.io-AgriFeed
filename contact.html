Frequently Asked Questions

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting our sales team directly, filling out the contact form on this page, or calling our main office. For large orders, we recommend scheduling a consultation with one of our regional sales managers.

What is your delivery area?

We currently deliver to all 50 states in the US. For international orders, please contact our sales team to discuss shipping options and requirements.

Do you offer samples of your products?

Yes, we offer sample sizes of most of our feed products for testing purposes. Contact your regional sales representative to request samples for your farm or operation.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, bank transfers, and checks. For established customers, we also offer net-30 payment terms upon approval.
